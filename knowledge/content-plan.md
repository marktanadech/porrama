# Porrama Website Content Plan

## Launch Goal

Create an English-first brochure website for future clients who need a reliable construction partner in Thailand. The first version should make Porrama look credible quickly, show relevant past experience, and guide visitors toward phone or email contact.

## Page Structure

1. Hero
   - Headline: Office Fit-Out & Interior Specialist
   - Support copy: Porrama Engineering delivers turnkey office fit-out, renovation, and construction services across Thailand with disciplined project management, quality control, and value engineering.
   - Primary CTA: Call 084-646-5997
   - Secondary CTA: Email Porrama

2. Expertise
   - Office fit-out and renovation
   - Steel and civil construction
   - Project management
   - Value engineering

3. Featured Projects
   - Corteva Agriscience Office Renovation
   - STT Parking Shelter
   - BTS Green Line Depot Facility Improvement
   - Servcorp Mercury Office Fit-Out
   - UNOPS Office Interior Decoration
   - Auto Alliance Employee Car Park

4. Client Trust
   - Use client names as text for v1.
   - Publish logos only after public-use approval.

5. About and Capability
   - Explain the turnkey approach: planning, coordination, construction, quality control, handover.
   - Mention breadth of work across corporate offices, international organizations, factories, automotive sites, transport facilities, and hospitality spaces.

6. Contact
   - Phone, email, and Bangkok address.
   - No form in v1.

## Design Direction

Use a professional construction-services tone: clean, structured, trustworthy, and image-led. Keep Porrama green as the brand anchor, add warm gold accents from the logo, and use project photography as the main visual proof. Avoid decorative gimmicks and keep the page practical for business visitors.
